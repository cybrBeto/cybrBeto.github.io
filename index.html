Intro

I was born in Los Angeles, California, but I grew up in Mexico City. From there, my family moved around a good bit in Mexico before finally settling in Houston, Texas. From there, I went to high school at Seven Lakes H.S. and then started my college career at Texas A&M. I participated in a program called the Engineering Academies.


My time at A&M has been more than amazing. From being able to start my own organization (Aggie Lemon Racing) to developing my career, I couldn't ask for more

Career Goals

Starting college, like many people, I felt lost. In reality, I didn't know what I wanted to do for my career until I entered my senior year. That is when I took my CYBR 405 class titled Digital Forensics and Incident response. It is currently taught by John Romero, a great professor who was also kind enough to be my senior design project's sponsor and advisor. He has now become an amazing mentor to me. You can find his LinkedIn here


From this course, I have branched out into the vast world of cybersecurity and I now know that I would love to be a part of it. Specifically, I am interested in penetration testing and incident response.
